Arroz con Leche

descripcion

This arroz con leche recipe is “great to warm you up and a perfect comfort dish,” according to recipe developer Kathryn Standing.

Ingredientes Arroz con Leche

  • Water: Cook the rice in two cups of water.
  • Cinnamon: Cook the rice with cinnamon sticks. Plus, use ground cinnamon to garnish.
  • Rice: You’ll need a cup of uncooked white rice. Short grain rice will yield a thicker and creamier final dish, according to Kathryn.
  • Milks: This creamy arroz con leche recipe calls for three types of milk:
  • whole milk, evaporated milk, and sweetened condensed milk.

pasos

  1. Bring the water and cinnamon stick to a boil, then simmer
  2. Add the rice, bring to a boil, and simmer until the water is absorbed
  3. Stir in the milk, evaporated milk, and sweetened condensed milk.
  4. Garnish with cinnamon for serving
